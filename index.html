Our Contribution towards Covid-19

Family survival kits which include basic rations

Our NGO provide basic necessities things to all poor families or the one who are in need of that because at that time condition of all of us was very critical either rich , middle or poor and no one is satisfied in helping each other.

Protective hygiene kits

We provide kits including face mask, sanitizers, tissue paper , handwash , medicines etc. to ensure their safety and protection.

Providing Free meal

We facilitates free meal to those peoples who are homeless or who need help and feeling hunger as due to lack of money as many people became jobless at that time due to lockdown and fast spreading of covid virus.

Facilitating supply of oxygen and provide medical services

We provide oxygen to various government and private hospitals. As Medical oxygen is an essential medicine in the treatment of covid-19 patients.

Proving transport in lockdown

We also allow transport facilities for those who are very far from their families due to strict lockdown.

Supporting people with disabilities

We provide support to disabled person who are suffering from covid and feel helpless as there is no one who are with them.
