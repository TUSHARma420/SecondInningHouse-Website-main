Our contribution towards the community

Women Safety and Empowerment

Our NGO plays a very vital role towards women safety and their empowerment by providing basic education ,provide protection for women and organize self-awareness programs. It is mainly concerned with the main aim of upliftment of women in our society. Women is the main focus of our NGO.

Providing facilities to specially abled people

We help to develop the capablilites of abled people. Provide guidance and develop awareness .Some facilities are Scholarship scheme provided for students with disabilities, Unemployment allowance to educated disabled persons, Provide empowerment of persons with disabilities.

Help Elderly People

We offer the best medical care of the elder persons. Our main focus is providing happiness and comfort which is much needed by the elders . We provide the best medical treatment and take proper care of the elderly people with the main aim of providing concern ,comfort and happiness which is much needed by the elders.

To educate every last child especially girls

As every child , doesn’t matter who they are and from where they come from deserves access to education. To educate the child for the adoption of good norms of a good citizenship .We work on a mission to provide standard education to the unprivileged children .

Organizing Activitites

We conduct activities on a monthly ,or sometimes on a yearly basis to donate or provide the people basic need of food, clothes etc. Our NGO can work to promote social on the large scale. It plays a critical part in developing society , improving communities and promote our citizebn participation .

Providing training and skills development

Our Efforts by NGOs focus on the empowerment of poor women. We provide training and development to all the women with the main aim to train and offer them with the opportunities of self Employment to improve their economic and social status.

About Us

Founded in 2014 as Community based organization seeking donor support for enriching the lives of the people who are old age people by making them self relevant and opening opportunity for Livelihood.
